National Childcare Scheme

Please be advised that the new National Childcare Scheme will be available in October.

Check out this website to see if you qualify under the income thresholds of eligibility. If you do qualify then in order to apply for the NCS subsidy you will need to have set up Verified MyGovID.

You will also need your Public Services Card

Parents! If you are interested in this scheme apply for your PPS card and Verified MygovID now, so that you will be ready when the scheme opens.

Rates

Rathduff Community Out Of School Service offers affordable childcare for students who attend Rathduff N.S.

We are trying to help parents plan ahead and so we wish to advise you of the following:

Our rates in September will be as follows:

Mornings

7:30 until 9:10 = €7(breakfast included)

8:30 until 9:10 = €5

Afternoons: €6 hourly

Due to staff:children ratio restrictions the maximum number of children we can cater for in the morning is 36, so places can be booked in advance and places are on a first come/first served basis.

The maximum number we can cater for in the afternoon will be 50, so we will operate a booking system then also.

Bookings can be made with Ger for a week ahead by phone (086) 248 2939 or when your collecting your child, book them in for following week.
